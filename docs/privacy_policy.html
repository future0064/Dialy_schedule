📋 Daily Schedule Privacy First

Chrome Extension · Last updated: April 15, 2026

Overview

Daily Schedule is a Chrome extension that helps you manage your daily tasks. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This page explains what data the extension handles and how.

Data Collection

We collect no data whatsoever. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected.
  • No usage analytics or tracking.
  • No data is transmitted to any server.
  • No third-party services are used.

Data Storage

All task data and your language preference are stored locally on your device using the Chrome storage.local API. This data:

  • Never leaves your browser.
  • Is never shared with anyone, including the developer.
  • Can be deleted at any time by removing the extension or clearing extension data.

Permissions

The extension requests only one permission:

  • storage — used solely to save your tasks and language preference locally on your device.

Sponsorship

The extension includes an optional link to a sponsorship page (Ko-fi). Clicking this link opens an external website in a new tab. No data is passed to that site from the extension.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new "last updated" date. The extension version number will also be incremented.
